How this watchlist is calculated:

    Rows are sorted by exposed lapses: expiring paid memberships multiplied by churn rate.
  • Churn
    Churn rate equals lapsed paid memberships divided by expiring paid memberships in the selected month.
  • Action
    High-risk and low-usage rows should trigger direct renewal outreach before package end dates.
